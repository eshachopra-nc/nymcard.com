## Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how NymCard uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. We use cookies to ensure the site functions correctly, improve performance, remember your preferences (such as language or country), and deliver content that may be relevant to you.

## What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow recognition of devices and storage of preference information. Cookies function as either session cookies (expiring upon browser closure) or persistent cookies (remaining stored on devices for future visits).

## Types of cookies we use

**Essential cookies.** These are required for proper website functionality, enabling core features like page navigation and secure access. Without them, the website may not work as intended.

**Preference cookies.** These remember user choices, such as selected country or language, preventing the need to reset them on repeat visits.

**Analytics cookies.** These help understand visitor behavior and improve performance by identifying frequently visited pages and user errors. Information is aggregated and does not personally identify individuals.

## Third-party cookies

Some cookies may be placed by trusted third parties, such as Google Analytics and Google Ads, to help us analyse site usage and improve user experience.

## Managing your cookies

Users can manage cookie preferences through the cookie banner or browser settings. Most browsers allow blocking or deleting cookies, though disabling essential or preference cookies may limit site functionality.

## Updates to this policy

The policy may be updated to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or business practices.
