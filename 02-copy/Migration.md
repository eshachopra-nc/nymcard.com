## Why NymCard

### Headline

Built to modernise. Designed to migrate.

### Body

Most platforms treat migration as a one-time project. NymCard treats it as part of the platform.

### Pillar 1

#### nCore is built for migration

Migration runs natively within nCore. Portfolios can move in phases, run in parallel, and follow the migration approach that best fits your business. Existing cards can remain active throughout the transition, or be re-issued as part of a broader transformation program.

### Pillar 2

#### Agentic AI does the heavy lifting

Mapping, testing, reconciliation, and cutover planning are handled by AI agents working alongside your team. The work that traditionally consumed months of engineering effort becomes automated, reviewable, and controlled.

## What the agents do

### Headline

Automate the work. Keep the control.

### Automated Mapping

Agents analyse legacy schemas and map cardholder data, BIN ranges, tokens, and operational data into nCore.

### Testing & Reconciliation

Parallel processing and automated reconciliation ensure balances, positions, and transactions remain aligned throughout the migration.

### Cutover Planning

Agents sequence migrations into controlled batches, identify dependencies, and surface risks before they become issues.

### Human Oversight

Every recommendation remains visible, reviewable, and subject to approval by your team.

## How a migration runs

### Headline

Five phases. One live program.

### Discovery

Assess the portfolio, architecture, and migration approach.

### Mapping

Transform and validate data against the nCore model.

### Parallel Run

Run nCore alongside the existing processor and reconcile continuously.

### Phased Cutover

Move customers in controlled batches with rollback available throughout.

### Live on nCore

Retire legacy infrastructure and operate on the full stack.

### Supporting Line

Your customers continue using the program throughout the transition.
