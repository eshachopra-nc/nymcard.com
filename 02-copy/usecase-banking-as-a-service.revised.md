- AI claims limited to fraud, risk, decisioning, and service, plus explainable and auditable.
- PCI DSS compliant (never a level). ISO 27001. Visa and Mastercard listed as separate principal-member items.
- Stats reuse only approved figures: 99.99% uptime, <2s processing, 1,000+ APIs, 135+ currencies.

Distinct from the homepage (no "one customer record / one platform" spine) and from the sibling use-case pages (leads on the incumbent's paradox and the connect-to-core beat, which no sibling runs).
