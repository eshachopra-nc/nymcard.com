# AEO / FAQ (schema markup, not rendered as visible page section)

**Section heading (for schema):**

Frequently asked questions about NymCard Stablecoin Settlement.

**FAQ items:**

**Q: What is stablecoin settlement infrastructure?**

A: Stablecoin settlement infrastructure is the technology layer that enables regulated financial institutions to execute cross-border settlement, liquidity coordination, and capital optimization using blockchain-based rails integrated with traditional payment infrastructure. NymCard's Stablecoin Settlement product runs on nCore, NymCard's full-stack payments infrastructure platform.

**Q: Is this a crypto product?**

A: No. Stablecoin Settlement is settlement infrastructure for regulated institutions, not a crypto trading platform, exchange, or consumer digital asset service. Stablecoin is the mechanism; regulated settlement is the product.

**Q: How is the product regulated?**

A: NymCard operates as a regulated infrastructure provider. The platform is PCI DSS Level 1 certified and ISO 27001 certified. Stablecoin Settlement is deployed within a controlled, compliance-aware framework with AML screening, sanctions controls, and regulatory reporting embedded at the infrastructure level.

**Q: How fast is settlement?**

A: Stablecoin Settlement is near real-time, depending on corridor availability and regulatory requirements. It moves settlement beyond SWIFT-driven batch cycles, but specific settlement times vary by corridor and rail.

**Q: How does this integrate with traditional payment rails?**

A: Stablecoin Settlement runs on the same nCore platform as Card Issuing and Money Movement, with one unified architecture for traditional scheme settlement and stablecoin-based settlement. No separate blockchain system is required.

**Q: What deployment models are available?**

A: Cloud, on-soil, and on-premise are all native deployment models, designed for institutional deployment.
