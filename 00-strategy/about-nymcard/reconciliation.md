## What it is (confirmed)
The reconciliation layer of nCore — automated reconciliation of transactions, settlements, and accounts across NymCard products and external systems. Before becoming its own layer it was a capability embedded across Cards, Settlement, and other products; it was promoted to a standalone layer on rising demand for automated reconciliation as a distinct offering.
