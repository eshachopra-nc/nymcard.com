# Decision: Rename nCore's AI intelligence layer to "NymAI Layer"

**Date:** 2026-06-05
**Status:** proposed · accepted · superseded

## Context
The nCore stack currently labels its top tier the "AI intelligence layer." We want naming that is branded, ownable, and makes the AI positioning explicit on the homepage nCore reveal.

## Options considered
1. Keep "AI intelligence layer" (generic, not ownable)
2. "NymAI Layer" (branded, ties AI directly to NymCard)

## Decision
Rename the AI intelligence layer to **"NymAI Layer"**.

## Why
A branded name is ownable and reinforces that nCore is AI-native rather than describing a generic capability. Supports the broader push to explicitly reference AI in the nCore reveal and the Full-Stack launch positioning.

## Consequences
- Update the [[Homepage]] nCore reveal copy and any stack diagrams to use "NymAI Layer".
- Check naming consistency against [[product-naming-comparison]].
- Revisit if it clashes with other product names or VARA/Lynq positioning.
